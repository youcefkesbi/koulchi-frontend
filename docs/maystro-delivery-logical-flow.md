# Maystro Delivery Integration Guide

## 🚚 **MAYSTRO DELIVERY - COMPLETE FLOW (NO CODE)**

---

## 🎯 **WHAT MAYSTRO ACTUALLY IS**

**Maystro = Delivery Management Platform**
- **Manages delivery drivers** in their network
- **Tracks deliveries** in real-time
- **Sends notifications** to customers
- **Handles delivery logistics** and coordination

---

## 🔄 **COMPLETE FLOW - STEP BY STEP**

### **PHASE 1: STORE OWNER SETUP (One-time)**

#### **Step 1: Store Owner Registers**
- **Goes to your e-commerce website**
- **Clicks "Enable Maystro Delivery"**
- **Fills registration form** (store name, address, contact)
- **Creates Maystro account**

#### **Step 2: Store Owner Gets Credentials**
- **Receives API token** from Maystro
- **Gets store ID** for their account
- **Access to Maystro dashboard**

#### **Step 3: Store Owner Configures Settings**
- **Sets delivery zones** (which areas they deliver to)
- **Sets delivery fees** (how much to charge customers)
- **Sets delivery times** (when deliveries are available)

---

### **PHASE 2: CUSTOMER PLACES ORDER**

#### **Step 1: Customer Shops**
- **Browses products** on your website
- **Adds items to cart**
- **Proceeds to checkout**

#### **Step 2: Customer Sees Delivery Options**
- **Standard Delivery** (3-5 days) - $5
- **Express Delivery** (1-2 days) - $10
- **Same Day Delivery** (if available) - $15

#### **Step 3: Customer Chooses Delivery**
- **Selects delivery option**
- **Enters shipping address**
- **Enters phone number**
- **Completes payment**

---

### **PHASE 3: ORDER PROCESSING**

#### **Step 1: Order Goes to Store Owner**
- **Store owner gets notification** of new order
- **Prepares the products** for delivery
- **Packages items** securely

#### **Step 2: Store Owner Sends to Maystro**
- **Logs into Maystro dashboard**
- **Creates delivery request** with order details
- **Schedules pickup** with Maystro driver

#### **Step 3: Maystro Assigns Driver**
- **Maystro finds available driver** in the area
- **Sends pickup notification** to driver
- **Driver goes to store** to collect package

---

### **PHASE 4: DELIVERY EXECUTION**

#### **Step 1: Driver Picks Up Package**
- **Driver arrives at store**
- **Collects packaged order**
- **Scans package** for tracking
- **Confirms pickup** in Maystro system

#### **Step 2: Driver Delivers to Customer**
- **Driver navigates to customer address**
- **Calls customer** when nearby
- **Delivers package** to customer
- **Gets delivery confirmation** from customer

#### **Step 3: Delivery Complete**
- **Driver marks delivery as complete**
- **Maystro sends confirmation** to store owner
- **Customer receives delivery notification**
